NpCoin

The Goal is to explore the cryptographic primitives used in bitcoin. Bitcoin is written in C and C++, but since most of the work I'm doing currently is in python and flask, I will be exploring these primitives in those languages and frameworks. To be clear, this is not a real cryptocurrency, its just a learning exercise to make a functioning "dumb copy" of the bitcoin protocol.
